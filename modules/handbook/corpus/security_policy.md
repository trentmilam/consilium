# Information Security Policy

All employees must enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) on every company account. Passwords must be at least twelve characters and are managed through the approved password manager.

Client personal data and other confidential information must never be sent to external or third-party online services that have not been approved by the security team.

Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled, and any lost or stolen device must be reported to IT security within twenty-four (24) hours.
