# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

Full-time employees accrue twenty (20) days of paid time off per year, accrued monthly at a rate of 1.67 days per completed month of service.

Unused PTO may be carried over into the following calendar year up to a maximum of five (5) days. Any balance above the carryover cap is forfeited at year end.

PTO requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance for planned absences, subject to manager approval and team coverage.
