# Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees are reimbursed for reasonable, business-related expenses. Meals while traveling are reimbursed up to a per diem of seventy-five dollars ($75) per day.

Itemized receipts are required for any single expense over twenty-five dollars ($25). Expenses submitted without a required receipt will not be reimbursed.

Approved expense reports are reimbursed through payroll within thirty (30) days of submission. Airfare and hotels should be booked through the company travel portal whenever possible.
