# Acme Corp - FY2025 Annual Report (10-K) excerpt

Acme Corp reported total revenue of $4.2 billion for fiscal year 2025, an increase of 18% year over year, driven primarily by the Cloud Platform segment.

Revenue by segment: Cloud Platform contributed $2.1 billion and grew 34%, the fastest-growing segment; Hardware contributed $1.3 billion and grew 5%; Professional Services contributed $0.8 billion and was roughly flat.

Net income for the year was $520 million, up from $410 million the prior year, and the company ended the period with $1.9 billion in cash and short-term investments.

Management guidance for FY2026 projects revenue between $4.8 billion and $5.0 billion, assuming continued double-digit growth in the Cloud Platform segment.
