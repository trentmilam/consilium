# Globex Industries - Q3 FY2025 Earnings Release excerpt

Globex Industries reported third-quarter revenue of $1.1 billion, up 9% year over year, and diluted earnings per share (EPS) of $2.35, beating the consensus estimate of $2.18.

Operating margin expanded to 22% from 19% a year earlier, reflecting cost discipline and a favorable product mix in the Industrial Automation division.

The company raised its full-year revenue guidance to a range of $4.3 billion to $4.4 billion and announced a $500 million share-repurchase authorization.
