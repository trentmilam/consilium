# Master Services Agreement - key terms (template)

Intellectual Property: unless otherwise stated in a Statement of Work, all deliverables created specifically for the client become the property of the client upon full payment, while the service provider retains ownership of its pre-existing tools, libraries, and know-how.

Limitation of Liability: each party's total liability arising out of the Agreement is capped at the total fees paid by the client in the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential damages.

Governing Law: this Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles.
